Implementation:

Users:

  • To log in or create an account, a button will prompt a Vue component that will allow the user to input information, but if they would rather continue as a guest, then they can but with fewer features available.
  • Normal users will be able to create accounts with a username log-in, and their data will be saved in Firebase so that their data may be saved.
  • Hair stylists will also be able to create accounts in Firebase, but their personal data will be different than the normal users' data.
  • Since the user's data is saved in Firebase, they can go to their own profile and see their previous reviews and posts and edit those. Hair stylists can also go to their profile and see the reviews that they were tagged in.

    • Home Screen:

  • The initial page will be loaded in a Vue app and different buttons on a navigation bar will trigger different Vue components to display different data pulled through Node.
  • A feed of images of haircuts will be displayed through a v-for loop of images in the user photo submissions part of the Firebase data.
  • The user can filter images by various descriptions that will be in the Firebase for those images.
  • Each image will display the hair stylist and the user, and if those are clicked on, a Vue component will load their profile onto the page.
  • When a user goes to a hair stylist's profile, their available schedule is displayed, and one of us will be experimenting with that during the explore assignment. Google Maps will also display where the salon/barber shop is, and another one of us will work with that for explore.
  • User can add a review of somewhere they recently got their hair cut. If the hair stylist does not have a profile, then the user can add the address and name of the location on their own. This review along with an image will be stored in Firebase, and if the hair stylist does have a profile, then it will be added to their account in Firebase as well.

    Different Modes:

    Guest mode:

  • Guests can look at reviews of shops in the area.
  • They can look at images that people have submitted.
  • They cannot bookmark/save any places until they make an account and save that in their profile.
  • Certain features won’t be visible because they’re in guest mode - like saving salons, etc.

    • User mode:

  • Can see posts/reviews made by other people.
  • Can save hair salons.
  • Choose from available times that the stylists have and make an appointment there.
  • Stylist can accept or decline the offer.
  • View their haircut history.

    • Hair Stylist mode:

  • Stylists can set times that they are available.
  • They can make posts and and view content of other stylists, just like normal users.
  • They can manually add appointments to their calendar.

    • Admin mode:

  • Can delete posts or accounts.
  • Have to be appointed by other admins.
